IMPACT at New Opera Days Ostrava

IMPACT is the first act of an opera by Amir Shpilman and choreographer Shiran Eliaserov. Presented at New Opera Days Ostrava after its showcase in Berlin, the work expands the operatic form by combining large-scale orchestration with live stunt performance, exposing fragility and vulnerability at the intersection of music, motion, and risk.

Photo © Johan Planefeldt

Key Points

  • Opera that integrates orchestration with live film stunts
  • Cast uniting a soprano, stuntwoman, and rigging specialists as performers
  • Explores vulnerability, embodiment, and mortality through performance

Details

The 360° staging brought together soprano Katsiaryna Zhynhiarouskaya, stunt artist Helga Wretman, and rigging experts Alexander Kusmak and Marten Baum. Shpilman’s score intertwined orchestration, foley, and sound design, producing an immersive environment where body and sound fuse into a single organism.
