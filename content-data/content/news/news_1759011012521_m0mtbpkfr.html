World Premiere – Amir Shpilman: Kriat Shema

On March 22, 2025, the Berlin Philharmonie hosted the world premiere of Amir Shpilman’s Kriat Shema – Fantasy for Shofar and Orchestra. Performed by the Berlin Symphony Orchestra under conductor Igor Budinstein, the evening marked a rare collision of ancient ritual and contemporary symphonic sound.

The shofar, a raw and ancient voice, stood at the center of the work—its sound woven into complex orchestral textures. The premiere wasn’t just about a new piece: it was about hearing tradition collide with the future in one of the most iconic concert halls in Europe.

For us at ZSCORE.studio, it was a privilege to prepare the full score, conductor’s score, and performance materials for this debut—ensuring every detail translated seamlessly to the stage.
