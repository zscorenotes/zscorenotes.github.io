Orchestral work by Laure M. Hiendl, premiered at Donaueschinger Musiktage 2025. We were responsible for producing and delivering the full orchestral material, including full score, conductor score, individual parts, and printing & shipping.

The Challenge

Prepare orchestral material with extremely precise notation, ensuring clarity for conductor and performers while meeting festival deadlines.

Our Role

Engraving, formatting, and producing the full orchestral score, conductor score, and individual parts. Coordinated printing and delivery to ensure performers had materials ready for the premiere.

Key Responsibilities

  • • Full orchestral score engraving and formatting
  • • Conductor score preparation
  • • Individual parts preparation
  • • Printing and delivery to festival
