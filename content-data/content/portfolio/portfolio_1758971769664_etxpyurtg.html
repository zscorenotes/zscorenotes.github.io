Kriat Shema by Amir Shpilman

Fantasy for Shofar and Orchestra by Amir Shpilman, premiered at the Berlin Philharmonie. ZSCORE.studio, led by Parham Behzad, prepared the full score, conductor score, individual parts, and performance materials for this premiere.

The Challenge

Preparing orchestral materials that integrated the Shofar—a rare, non-standard instrument—while ensuring clarity across complex orchestral textures. High standards of readability and precision were required for the premiere setting.

Our Role

We engraved and formatted the full orchestral score, conductor score, and individual parts. In addition to digital preparation, we oversaw printing and delivery of the performance materials, ensuring that musicians and conductor had reliable resources in time for rehearsals and the premiere.

Key Responsibilities

  • • Full orchestral score engraving and formatting
  • • Conductor score preparation
  • • Individual parts preparation
  • • Printing and delivery of materials
