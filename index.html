ZSCORE delivers specialized services in score development, audio programming, and software engineering for the contemporary music landscape.

Our approach combines meticulous engraving with advanced automation, ensuring rapid turnaround and structural integrity for even the most demanding projects. From large-scale operas to interactive audio systems, ZSCORE provides precise technical execution that aligns with artistic vision.

Engineered by composers, for musical innovation. Efficient. Scalable. Precise.

Core Services

Automated Score Engraving & Editorial

High-precision digital engraving optimized for clarity and performance in complex contemporary notation. Services include part preparation, cue management, error correction, and layout optimization. Streamlined workflows support Sibelius, Lilypond, OpusModus, and MusicXML.

Engraving Automation Development

Custom software tools designed to automate score editing, layout, and formatting. These solutions minimize repetitive tasks, reduce error rates, and integrate seamlessly into existing production environments for composers and publishers.

Computer-Assisted Orchestration & Notation

Development of rule-based systems for rapid orchestration and structured scoring. These tools align with compositional logic, reducing manual orchestration time while preserving artistic control, ideal for large-scale works and tight deadlines.

Audio Programming & System Design

Modular real-time systems for live electronics, spatialization, and generative sound. Built in Max/MSP, Pure Data, and custom frameworks, adaptable for solo, chamber, and multichannel setups.

Custom Music Software Tools

Bespoke web-based and standalone applications for harmony, tuning, notation, and interactive musical experiences. Interfaces are intuitively designed for composers and performers, supporting experimentation and enhancing workflows using JavaScript and Python.

Technical Consulting & Content Creation

Expert problem-solving for notation systems, plugin development, and performance logistics. Specialized content creation for social media, including score animations and technical demonstrations, designed to present complex musical work effectively online.

About ZSCORE

ZSCORE was founded by Parham Behzad, a multidisciplinary composer, audio programmer, and software developer. With a foundation in both advanced composition and computer science, ZSCORE operates at the intersection of musical artistry and technological precision.

Our studio specializes in high-efficiency workflows for score preparation, custom software development for musical applications, and audio system design. We leverage automation and bespoke tooling to enhance accuracy, speed, and structural clarity in every project. ZSCORE has contributed to projects for internationally recognized composers and institutions, including Notengrafik Berlin and the Staatsoper Berlin.

The ethos of ZSCORE is to provide reliable, exact, and innovative solutions, enabling composers and musical organizations to realize complex artistic visions with technical excellence.
