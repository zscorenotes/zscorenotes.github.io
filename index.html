ZSCORE is a specialized studio for score engraving/editing, orchestration, audio programming, and custom tool development. We support composers, ensembles, and institutions working at the edge of contemporary music.

Our workflow integrates precision engraving with advanced automation, enabling fast turnaround and structural clarity even in the most complex projects. From large-scale operas to real-time audio systems, we provide exact technical execution aligned with your artistic intent.

Built by composers, for composers. Efficient. Scalable. Reliable.

Services

Score Engraving

High-precision digital engraving for contemporary works. Focus on clarity, structure, and performance readability. Supports advanced notation systems and large-scale formats. Output optimized for print and digital publication.

Score Editorial

Critical editing and refinement of scores and performance materials. Services include part preparation, cue managments, error correction, layout optimization, notation consistency, and integration of editorial guidelines. Suitable for new works, revised editions, and archival reconstruction. Precision-focused and performance-ready output for ensembles, publishers, and institutions.

Engraving and Editorial Automation

Custom-built tools to accelerate input, editing, layout, and formatting. Automation minimizes manual tasks and error rates. Designed for composers, editors, and publishers working with large volumes or tight deadlines.

Computer-Assisted Orchestration

Rule-based orchestration systems for translating sketches and concepts into structured scores. Ideal for complex instrumentation, dense textures, or rapid development cycles.

Audio Programming

Modular real-time systems for live electronics, spatialization, and generative sound. Designed for integration with composition and performance environments. Built using Max/MSP, Pure Data, and custom frameworks.

Custom Software Tools

Browser-based and standalone applications for notation, harmony, tuning, and interaction. Built with composers in mind. Focused on usability, precision, and workflow enhancement.

Technical Consulting

Problem-solving for notation systems, plugin development, score formatting, and performance logistics. Adaptable to project-specific needs. Efficient, code-level solutions.

Content Creating for Social Media

Design and production of specialized content for composers, performers, and institutions aiming to present complex musical work effectively online. Includes score-based animations, patch demonstrations, behind-the-scenes workflows, and process breakdowns. Focus on clarity, technical depth, and aesthetic precision. Optimized for platforms such as Instagram, YouTube, and Vimeo.

About

We are a collective engraving and editorial studio specialized in serving composers, orchestras, and ensembles working at the forefront of contemporary music. Our focus is on delivering high-efficiency workflows, structural clarity, and technical precision across all aspects of score preparation and production.

With a background in both advanced composition and software development, our team integrates automation and custom tooling into every stage of the editorial process—engraving, orchestration, layout, and audio integration—minimizing manual repetition while maximizing accuracy and interpretability.

Whether for complex chamber works, large-scale opera, electroacoustic setups, or institutional publishing, we provide reliable, fast, and exact solutions tailored to each project’s demands.
